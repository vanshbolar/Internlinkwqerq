ACCENTURE
DESCRIPTION
Accenture is a global professional services firm providing a broad range of services and solutions in strategy, consulting, digital, technology, and operations. Founded in 1989, Accenture has grown to become one of the largest consulting firms in the world, with operations spanning across more than 200 cities in over 120 countries. The company is renowned for its expertise in helping clients harness the power of technology and innovation to drive business transformation and achieve high performance. Accenture serves clients in various industries, including but not limited to, banking and financial services, healthcare, telecommunications, consumer goods, energy, and utilities.
